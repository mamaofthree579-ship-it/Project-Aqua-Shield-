# Family-Size Sand + Charcoal Bucket Filter
Fastest to scale for communities or households.

---

## Materials
- 2 buckets or jerrycans
- Clean sand (washed)
- Small gravel
- Wood charcoal (crushed)
- Cloth or mesh layer
- Spigot (optional) or small hole for drip

---

## Layer Order (Top → Bottom)
1. Cloth or mesh
2. Coarse gravel
3. Small gravel
4. Charcoal layer (5–8 cm)
5. Clean sand (15–25 cm)
6. Cloth layer at bottom

---

## Assembly
1. Make a small hole at bottom of bucket for water outlet.
2. Add layers in the order listed above.
3. Pour raw water slowly into top bucket.
4. Filtered water collects in lower container.

---

## Effectiveness
Removes:
- turbidity  
- many bacteria  
- odors  
- some chemicals  

Still requires boiling or chemical disinfection afterward.
