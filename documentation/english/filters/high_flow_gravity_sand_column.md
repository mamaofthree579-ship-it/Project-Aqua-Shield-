# High-Flow Gravity Sand Column (Tap Water Version)
**Purpose:** Fast gravity filter adapted for tap water that balances flow and filtration. Good for households that need decent flow while removing turbidity, microplastics, and improving taste when combined with charcoal.

---

## BOM
- 1–2 L plastic bottle or PVC column — 1  
- Microfiber prefilter or cloth — 1 piece  
- Fine sand — 1–2 cups  
- Crushed charcoal — ½ cup  
- Small gravel — ½ cup  
- Rubber band / tape — as needed  
**Estimated cost:** $1–$2

---

## ASCII Schematic
```
(Top opening) -> [Microfiber prefilter] -> (Charcoal layer) -> (Sand layer) -> (Gravel) -> (Cloth at neck) -> Drips into cup
```

---

## Assembly
1. Cut bottle bottom. Remove lid and cover mouth with cloth. Secure.  
2. Add gravel (bottom), sand (middle), charcoal (top), then microfiber or cloth at top opening as prefilter.  
3. Position bottle inverted over clean container.

---

## Use
- Fill from tap slowly; keep flow moderate to allow contact time with charcoal.  
- Works well for daily kitchen use as pre-drinking step (then disinfect).

---

## Maintenance
- Replace charcoal every 2–4 weeks.  
- Rinse sand monthly.  
- Replace cloth weekly or when torn.

---

## Safety & Limitations
- **Good for:** turbidity, microplastics, sediment, taste/odor.  
- **Not sufficient alone** for viruses or heavy contamination — use disinfection step afterwards.
