# Emergency Cloth-Only Water Filter
Used when no sand or charcoal is available.

---

## Materials
- Clean cotton cloth, scarf, or T-shirt
- Clean container

---

## Instructions
1. Fold cloth into **4–8 layers**.
2. Pour water slowly through cloth.
3. Repeat filtration 2–3 times.

---

## Notes
This removes:
- large particles  
- sediment  
- insects  
- some microorganisms

But does **not** disinfect water.

Always follow cloth filtration with:
- boiling 1 minute  
- or SODIS (solar disinfection)  
- or chlorine tablets
