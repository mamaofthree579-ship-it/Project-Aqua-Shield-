# Solar Water Disinfection (SODIS)
Zero-cost disinfection for low-resource or crisis conditions.

---

## Materials
- Clear PET plastic bottles (1–2 L)
- Sunlight

---

## Steps
1. Pre-filter water through cloth until visually clear.
2. Fill bottle ¾ full and shake 20 seconds.
3. Fill completely.
4. Lay bottle in direct sunlight on roof, rock, or ground.

Time needed:
- **6 hours in full sunlight**
- **2 days if cloudy**

---

## What SODIS Removes
- bacteria  
- viruses  
- many protozoa  

Does not remove chemicals or heavy metals.
