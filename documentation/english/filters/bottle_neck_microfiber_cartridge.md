# Bottle-Neck Microfiber + Carbon Cartridge (Reusable)
**Purpose:** Reusable cartridge made from bottle necks for households: reduces turbidity, microplastics, chlorine taste, and odors. Very cheap and scalable.

---

## BOM
- Several clean plastic bottle necks or short bottle sections — 1–2  
- Microfiber cloth or old microfiber cleaning cloth — small pieces  
- Crushed charcoal — ½ cup  
- Fine sand — 1/2 cup (optional)  
- Cotton or plug material — small amount  
**Estimated cost:** <$1 (use recycled bottles)

---

## Assembly
1. Cut a bottle at the neck or use the neck piece inverted.  
2. Insert a small cotton plug at the narrowest end to hold layers.  
3. Add 1–2 cm microfiber layer, then sand, then microfiber, then charcoal, then final microfiber/top cloth.  
4. Secure with tape or another bottle section to sandwich the cartridge.

---

## Use
- Invert cartridge over cup or place into funnel and pour slowly.  
- After first assembly, discard first 1–2 L to clean dust.

---

## Maintenance
- Rinse outer cloth daily.  
- Replace charcoal and sand every 2–6 weeks depending on turbidity.  
- Replace microfiber layers monthly or when clogged.

---

## Safety & Limitations
- **Good for:** turbidity, microplastics, taste/odor.  
- **Not for:** heavy metals, full disinfection. Always disinfect after filtering.
