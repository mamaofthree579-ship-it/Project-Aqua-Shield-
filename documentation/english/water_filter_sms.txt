BASIC SAFE WATER INSTRUCTIONS (SMS VERSION)

1. FILTER
Use cloth folded 4–8 layers. Pour water through slowly.
Repeat 2–3 times.

2. IMPROVE
If charcoal exists, crush it and put it in a bottle above sand.
Let water drip through.

3. DISINFECT
BEST: Boil 1 min.
OR: 1 drop bleach per cup (clear, unscented).
OR: SODIS (sunlight): leave clear bottles in sun 6 hrs.

If water smells like fuel, do NOT drink.
