Water Safety Decision Guide

## Step 1 — Look at Water
If water is:
- **Clear** → go to Step 2  
- **Cloudy** → cloth filter → sand/charcoal  
- **Smells like fuel/solvent** → DO NOT USE  

---

## Step 2 — Choose Treatment
If you have:
- **Sun** → SODIS  
- **Fuel** → Boil  
- **Chlorine tablets** → Disinfect  
- **None of the above** → Use best filter available & settle water  

---

## Step 3 — After Treatment
Store in:
- Covered container  
- Do not touch inside  
- Use clean cup or ladle  

---

## Step 4 — When to Seek Alternative Water
- Strong chemical smell  
- Metal taste  
- Unusual color (blue, red, yellow)  
- Oil film on surface
